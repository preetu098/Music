BioGraphy

Shreyash Shandiliya (Born 21st November, 1993) Is An Indian , Composer, Lyricist & Owner/Founder Of SoulFul Musik Academy & HS Musik Company. Shreyash Have Also Collaborated With Other Bollywood Singers Like Krishna Beura, Saraj Jagan, Anwesshaa, Shweta Pandit & Others.

Early Life

Shreyash Was Born On 21st November, 1993 In Amarpur, Banka, Bihar To Sujit Kumar Tiwary & Punam Tiwary. He Did His Schooling From Saint Joseph’s School, Banka. He Didn’t Enjoy Studying & Music Was Something Where He Was Always Attracted Towards. He Performed For The 1st Time In His School While He Was In 2nd Standard Where He Sang Tumse Achha Kaun Hai & Received 1st Prize & This Is How Kept On Participating From Then In His School & Town. He Was Strongly Influenced & Inspired By Himesh

Reshammiya’s Music & Singing Skills, He Still Have All The Audio Cassette Collection Of Himesh Reshammiya’s Almost All The Songs At His Mumbai Residence. He Also Went For An Audition For Indian Idol Junior & Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Little Champs Where He Couldn’t Make It As He Was Not At All Formally Trained. His Parents Asked Him To Start Learning With Some Teacher There In Banka, But He Wanted To Begin Everything In & From Mumbai. & Thus, Moved To Mumbai While He Was In 10th


Standard & He Received His 11th & 12th Degree’s From NIOS (National Institute Of Open Schooling)

Musical Career

He Was Here Just To Be A Singer, But With Almighty’s Deed He Was Blessed With Add On Skills To Write & Compose Songs & Started Singing & Writing Many Songs From 2011 Onwards.

At The Initial Days In Mumbai His Vocal’s Were Not That Up to The Mark To Do Playback Singing, His Guru Guided Him That He Can Be A Composer & Lyricist If His Composed Songs & Lyrics Will Sung Well Known Singer. Shreyash Kept On Working On Improving His Singing Skills & Along With That He Started Writing & Composing Songs & Started Recording Scratch Versions Of His Composed Songs Which He Can Make Other Singers Listen To It & Ask Them To Sing His Song.

He Contacted All Most Everyone Through Facebook Via Message, He Also Had A Conversation With Arijit Singh In 2013 Where He Had Talked To Him About His Success, He Owe His Career’s Success To Hard-work Or Almighty.

His Answer Gave Him Strong Belief On Working Hard & Almighty. He Initiated His Composition Topic, But Arijit Couldn’t Reply To It Because Of His Busy Schedules & Tons Of Other Messages. He Also Had A Conversation With Abhijeet Sawant Where Abhijeet Loved Shreyash’s Composition Tere Bin Banjar But That Conversation Also Didn’t Work Same Went With Monali Thakur & Many Others.

Finally Krishna Beura Loved His Composition Tujhse Milke Ye Baawra & Agreed To Meet & Record The Song & This How A Small Town’s Boy Shreyash Shandiliya’s Very 1st Song Was Sung By Krishna Beura (The Voice Behind Shah Rukh Khan’s Chack De India’s Maula Mere Lele Meri Jaan, Aap Ki Kashish, Main Jahaan Rahoon, Chaska & Many Other Hits) & The Song Was Made in 2013 But Mohnish Behl Lyricist Of The Song Tu Jo Hai To Main Hoon Sung By Ankit Tiwari He Informed To Him Him Some Lyrical Error Which Took Another 1 & Half Year To Re Contact & Fix The Date With Krishna Beuraa & Later After That Shreyash Also Decided To Recorded Female Version Of The Same Song Which Happened With Anwesshaa (The Voice Of India Contestant & Voice Behind Salman Khan’s Jaltey Diye & Papon’s Benaam Si Khwaahishein From Coke Studio) Now Both Male & Female Version Was Ready With Him & He Started Meeting Directors & Producers, Actor’s From Bollywood Like, Amole Gupte (Writer Of Taare Zameen Par) Vikramaditya Motwane (Director Of Ranveer Singh’s & Sonakshi Sinha’s Lootera) Actor Manoj Kumar & Many Other. But None Of The Meetings Worked Successful & The Song Remained At Home In His Bag.

Thus He Came To Conclusion That Waiting For Someone To Give Him Break & Some Popular Company To Release His Song Would Really Take A Lot Of Months Or Years. So He Formed His Own Music Company Named As HS Musik Company Where He Released His 1st Song On 14th February, 2016 Followed By Sholon Se Bhara With Suraj Jagan, Hey Ram With Shweta Pandit ( Hey Ram Also Mark’s As Shreyash’s 1st Song As A Singer Followed By Laut Aana Uss Jahaan Se, Keh Do Na Pyaar Hai, Khushnuma, Tere Bin Banjar, Baarish Hogi & More.
