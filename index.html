The Moto Of His Academy Is Not Just To Make Money As He Welcomes All, Who Even Can’t Afford The Fees. Shreyash Shandiliya (Born 21st November, 1993) Is An Indian Singer, Composer, Lyricist & Owner/Founder Of SoulFul Musik Academy & HS Musik Company.
